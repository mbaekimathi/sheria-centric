Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure Agreement

{{ company_settings.company_name }}

This Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between {{ company_settings.company_name }} ("Company") and the undersigned employee ("Employee") effective as of the date of employment commencement.

This Agreement is designed to protect the confidential and proprietary information of the Company and its clients. By accepting this agreement, you acknowledge your understanding and commitment to maintain the highest standards of confidentiality.

1 Definition of Confidential Information

For purposes of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:

  • Client information, case details, and legal strategies
  • Financial information, business plans, and proprietary processes
  • Employee information, salary data, and personnel records
  • Technical information, software, and system architectures
  • Marketing strategies, client lists, and business relationships
  • Any information marked as "Confidential" or "Proprietary"
  • Any information that would be considered confidential by a reasonable person

2 Obligations of Employee

The Employee agrees to:

  • Maintain Confidentiality: Not disclose, use, or permit others to use any Confidential Information except as necessary in the performance of job duties
  • Protect Information: Take all reasonable precautions to protect Confidential Information from unauthorized access or disclosure
  • Return Materials: Upon termination of employment, return all documents, files, and materials containing Confidential Information
  • Report Breaches: Immediately report any suspected or actual breach of confidentiality to management
  • Comply with Policies: Follow all company policies and procedures regarding information security and data protection

3 Exceptions

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

  • Was publicly known or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement
  • Was rightfully known by the Employee prior to employment
  • Is independently developed by the Employee without use of Confidential Information
  • Is required to be disclosed by law or court order (with prior notice to Company when possible)

4 Duration of Obligations

The obligations under this Agreement shall continue:

  • During the entire period of employment
  • For a period of three (3) years following termination of employment
  • Indefinitely for trade secrets and proprietary information that remains confidential

5 Consequences of Breach

Any breach of this Agreement may result in:

  • Immediate termination of employment
  • Legal action for damages and injunctive relief
  • Criminal prosecution if applicable under relevant laws
  • Liability for all costs and expenses incurred by the Company in enforcing this Agreement

6 Acknowledgment

By accepting this Agreement, the Employee acknowledges that:

  • They have read and understood all terms and conditions
  • They have had the opportunity to seek legal counsel if desired
  • They agree to be bound by all provisions of this Agreement
  • They understand the serious consequences of breaching this Agreement

Effective Date: Upon acceptance during onboarding

Status: This agreement remains in effect until modified or terminated by mutual written consent.
