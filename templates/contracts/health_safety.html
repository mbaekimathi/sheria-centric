Health & Safety Declaration

{{ company_settings.company_name }}

This Health & Safety Declaration ("Declaration") establishes the commitment of {{ company_settings.company_name }} and its employees to maintain a safe and healthy work environment. This Declaration reflects our shared responsibility for workplace safety and well-being.

All employees are expected to read, understand, and comply with this Declaration. Your safety and well-being are our top priorities.

1 Company Commitment

{{ company_settings.company_name }} is committed to:

  • Safe Work Environment: Providing a safe and healthy workplace free from recognized hazards
  • Safety Training: Providing appropriate safety training and resources to all employees
  • Equipment Safety: Maintaining safe equipment and facilities
  • Emergency Preparedness: Establishing and maintaining emergency response procedures
  • Compliance: Complying with all applicable health and safety laws and regulations
  • Continuous Improvement: Regularly reviewing and improving safety policies and procedures

2 Employee Responsibilities

All employees are responsible for:

  • Following Safety Procedures: Adhering to all safety policies, procedures, and instructions
  • Using Safety Equipment: Using provided safety equipment and personal protective equipment (PPE) as required
  • Reporting Hazards: Immediately reporting any unsafe conditions, hazards, or incidents to management
  • No Unsafe Acts: Not engaging in any activity that could endanger themselves or others
  • Participating in Training: Attending required safety training sessions and staying informed about safety matters
  • Maintaining Cleanliness: Keeping work areas clean, organized, and free from hazards

3 Workplace Hazards

Employees must be aware of and report:

  • Physical Hazards: Slippery floors, exposed wires, damaged equipment, or unsafe structures
  • Ergonomic Issues: Improper workstation setup, repetitive motion risks, or lifting hazards
  • Chemical Hazards: Improperly stored or labeled chemicals, spills, or exposure risks
  • Fire Hazards: Blocked exits, faulty electrical equipment, or improper storage of flammable materials
  • Health Risks: Poor air quality, excessive noise, or other environmental health concerns

4 Emergency Procedures

All employees must:

  • Know Emergency Exits: Be familiar with all emergency exits and evacuation routes
  • Understand Alarm Systems: Know how to recognize and respond to fire alarms and other emergency signals
  • First Aid: Know the location of first aid kits and how to contact emergency services
  • Evacuation Procedures: Follow established evacuation procedures in case of emergency
  • Emergency Contacts: Know how to contact emergency services and company emergency contacts

5 Health & Wellness

The company supports employee health and wellness through:

  • Ergonomic Support: Providing ergonomic assessments and equipment to prevent workplace injuries
  • Mental Health: Supporting mental health and providing resources for stress management
  • Work-Life Balance: Encouraging healthy work-life balance and reasonable working hours
  • Health Resources: Providing access to health information and wellness programs
  • Accommodations: Providing reasonable accommodations for employees with health conditions or disabilities

6 Incident Reporting

All employees must:

  • Report Immediately: Report all accidents, injuries, or near-misses immediately to management
  • Document Incidents: Complete incident reports accurately and completely
  • Seek Medical Attention: Seek appropriate medical attention for any work-related injury or illness
  • Cooperate with Investigations: Cooperate fully with any investigation of safety incidents
  • No Retaliation: Understand that reporting safety concerns will not result in retaliation

7 Acknowledgment

By accepting this Declaration, I acknowledge that:

  • I have read and understood this Health & Safety Declaration
  • I understand my responsibilities for workplace safety
  • I will follow all safety procedures and report hazards promptly
  • I will participate in safety training and comply with safety requirements
  • I understand that safety is a shared responsibility

Effective Date: Upon acceptance during onboarding

Company: {{ company_settings.company_name }}

Emergency Contact: Contact HR or management immediately for any safety concerns

Review Period: This Declaration is reviewed regularly and updated as needed to ensure continued safety.
